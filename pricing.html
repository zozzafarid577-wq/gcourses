Why Gcourses Exists

Biology is
everywhere.

The resources already exist. YouTube, Barron's, past papers, textbooks — they're all out there. But they're scattered, unorganized, and built for no one in particular.

Gcourses isn't about gatekeeping knowledge. It's about giving you the structure and clarity to use what's already available — effectively, efficiently, and on your own terms.

The eBook was built so a student can open it, follow the plan, and walk into the exam prepared — without needing a teacher. The sessions exist for students who want guidance alongside that.

What's the difference between the eBook and sessions?
The eBook is for independent study — everything you need to study alone. Sessions are for students who want Gigi to walk them through the material live, one chapter at a time.
How many sessions will I need?
It depends on your starting point and exam date. You can book 1 session to try it out, or ask Gigi on WhatsApp for a full course plan.
Does the eBook work for both ACT and EST?
Yes — the curriculum is identical for both. The revision materials and past papers cover both exams.
How do I pay right now?
Through the pre-order form — Gigi will confirm your order manually. Paymob (card, Instapay, wallet) is coming soon.
Can I get the eBook AND sessions?
Absolutely — and that's the most effective combo. Ask Gigi on WhatsApp for a bundled plan.
How do I book a session?
Fill out the enrollment form and Gigi will reach out to schedule at a time that works for you.
