Delivery & Shipping Policy

Last updated: 15 June 2026

1. Digital products — no physical shipping

Gcourses sells digital products and online services only. There is nothing to physically ship, so there are no shipping fees and no delivery address required. Everything is delivered electronically to the email address you use at checkout.

2. The Complete eBook

  • After your payment is confirmed, access is delivered instantly and automatically — typically within a few minutes.
  • You will receive an email at the address used at checkout with a secure login link to the online study portal, where you can read and download the eBook.
  • Access is lifetime — you can return to the portal whenever you like by requesting a new login link.

3. 1-on-1 Sessions

  • After booking, we contact you by email or WhatsApp to schedule your session at a time that works for you.
  • Sessions are held online. You'll receive the meeting link before your session start time.

4. Delivery timeframe

Digital access is normally granted immediately after a successful payment. In the rare case of a delay (for example, while a payment is being verified), access is delivered within 24 hours. If you have not received your access within that window, please contact us.

5. Shipping fees

There are no shipping or handling fees. The price you see at checkout is the full price.

6. Didn't receive your access?

If you completed payment but didn't get your access email, please:

  • Check your spam / promotions folder,
  • Confirm you entered the correct email address at checkout, and
  • Contact us — we'll resend your access right away.
